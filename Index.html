About the Project

By Samantha Jegglie


Project Description

Design and develop a visually pleasing, interactive website with multiple HTML pages, navigation connecting all the pages, featuring a cohesive color scheme, a clear visual hierarchy, a balanced layout, exciting visuals, and text. The goal is to learn how to code/develop an interactive website by applying design principles.

About the Designer

Samantha is a Graphic Design major at North Central College in Naperville, IL. She has experience working with Creative Cloud applications such as Photoshop, InDesign, Illustrator, XD, Dreamweaver, After Effects, and Animate. She is also interested in studio art, drawing, and photography.

Samantha is on track to graduate in the Spring of 2023 but until then she hopes to be able to share her love and passion for design with the world through internships and volunteer work. During the summer of 2022, she was a marketing design intern for Collegis Education in Oakbrook, IL where she helped with rebranding their materials for the company. During the school year, she works for the marketing department at North Central College as a graphic designer where she produces a variety of content to be used to market the college. She also works as a creative director for the student-led design agency at North Central. She acquired her Bachelors in Elementary Education where she works at schools in the area as a substitute teacher when she is not on campus. As a student, she was a summer school art teacher for an internship through North Central College for high needs schools where she was able to share her love of art with her students. At her previous college, Illinois State University, she worked for University Housing creating marketing materials for their campus. She has always had a passion for art as a way to express through through different mediums.
